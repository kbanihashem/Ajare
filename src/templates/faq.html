Frequently Asked Questions

How will the console be delivered to me?

After requesting a console, we ask for your address; We offer free shipping for all of the products.

Can I connect to the console by my own PlayStation account?

You are free to use your own PSN account on the device. However, the account must belong to you. We encourage you to sign out of your account before returning the console, also delete any games that you might download via your account.
