Introduction

Do you want to have a gaming session with your friends, but don't have a console?

Are you tired of wasting your time going to a gamenet to play their limited choice of games? What if you could have your friends come over at the comfort of your home and play with ease of mind?

This is what Ajare is all about! You don't need to worry about meeting up at a public place in this time of crisis; You can have a fun time at cleanliness and comfort of your home.

How does it work?

You can easily rent a console and have it delivered to you. You can select your desired games to be installed on the console you order. We also take all the consoles delivered to customers into a disinfection process, so you won't need to worry about health issues.

You get to choose the length of the period you want to use the console; We offer great prices for any amount of time!
